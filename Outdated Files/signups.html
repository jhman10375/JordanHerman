FallFest

KIO Louisville “Drew Corson” Memorial Basketball Tournament and BBG Sisterhood Weekend


Description: Join BBYO Columbus as we travel to Louisville to take part in the annual Drew Corson Memorial Basketball Tournament and BBG Sisterhood weekend. We will join Cincinnati, Louisville, Indianapolis, and the rest of KIO for a weekend of friendly competition and fun within the KIO family.

Who: All BBYO members in 9th-12th grade

Not a member and want to join? If so, click here to join BBYO

When: Friday November 10th & Saturday November 11th, We will be taking a bus to Louisville, leaving after school on November 10th and will return the evening of Saturday November 11th

Location: Louisville JCC (3600 Dutchmans Ln, Louisville, KY 40205)

Click Here to register for Louisville Basketball and BBG Sisterhood Weekend
Registration Deadline is Sunday November 5th!

BBYO Presents: An Exclusive Pre-Screening Of The Movie “Justice League” – Thursday November 16th 6:00 PM


Description: BBYO Columbus invites you to be the first to see the upcoming hit movie “Justice League” during our exclusive Fallfest prescreening on Thursday November 16 at the AMC movie theater in Easton! There, we will have a whole theater to ourselves to see what happens as our favorite Israeli Actress Gal Gadot (who plays Wonder Woman) teams up with Superman, Batman, Aquaman, The Flash, and others to save the world! For this program, BBYO is partnering with the Muscular Dystrophy Association, to invite their members to join us for the movie, and to help us learn more about a disease that afflicts numerous people both within and outside our Columbus community, including ways we can help!

Who: All Jewish Teens in Columbus in 7th-12th grade (you do not need to be a BBYO member to attend this event)

When: Thursday November 16th, doors open at 6:00 PM

Location: AMC Easton Town Center 30 (275 Easton Station, Columbus, OH 43219)

Click Here to register and secure your ticket for “Justice League”
Tickets are available on a first come, first serve basis!


Aliah BBG Cyclebar Fundraiser – Sunday November 19th 12:30 PM


Description: Aliah BBG will be holding an exciting fundraiser at Cyclebar in Easton in support of the Muscular Dystrophy Association, leukemia research, and for their chapter to raise money for awesome programming throughout the year. We invite BBYO Columbus members, parents, siblings, friends, and grandparents to take part and help our local BBG chapter make a huge impact on the 19th!

Who: Everyone who wants to come support the causes!

When: Sunday November 19th 12:30 PM

Location: Cyclebar Easton (4201 Weaverton Lane, Columbus, OH 43219)
Stay tuned: registration coming soon!


AZAA Basketball Weekend at Indiana University – Friday December 1st to Sunday December 3rd


Description: Join us as we travel to Indiana University in Bloomington, IN, home of the Indiana Hoosiers, winners of 5 NCAA Men’s Basketball National Championships, as Alephs from KIO, GMR, Miami, NRE: DC Council, MAR: St. Louis and CSR: Nashville unite for a weekend of basketball, brotherhood and an opportunity to explore the IU campus, hear from admissions staff, and connect with campus partners such as Hillel, AEPi and the Borns Jewish Studies Department.

Who: All AZA members in 9th-12th grade
Not a member and want to join? If so, click here to join BBYO

When: Friday December 1st to Sunday December 3rd

Location: Bloomington, Indiana

Click Here to register for the AZAA basketball weekend at IU

Registration Deadline is Sunday November 19th!

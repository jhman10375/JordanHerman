The Jewish Community Center of Greater Columbus

The Jewish Community Center of Greater Columbus (JCC) is a community center for families accross Columbus.

Ropes Course Director

In the summer of 2021 I was brought back to the JCC as a senior counselor responsible for assisting with all ropes course related functions and activities for the season. This began with an intense training where we learned the ins and out of the course, safety plans, and emergency procedures. At the conclusion of this training I successfully completed dozens of practice rescues of my staff which would ensure that I would be prepared when faced with a real emergency with your children. In addition to the practice rescues, we took a written examination and based on my scores I was named Ropes Course Director where it would be my job to ensure the best practices and safety checks were completed in addition to creating new trainings for my staff, and educating our kids on how to be on the course safely. Outside of my course responsibility, as my role would be to challenge our kids at a new level I was also responsible for creating and leading teambuilding and leadership activities for the kids.
